Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL

Why is it only $49.99?

Treedi uses a modern and efficient care model that reduces administrative costs and unnecessary clinic visits. This allows us to keep consultations affordable while maintaining high medical standards.

GENERAL

What are your hours?

Treedi consultations are available Monday to Sunday from 12 PM to midnight.

GENERAL

How does the consultation work?

You start by chatting with our support team and describing your symptoms. Your case is then reviewed by a licensed physician who determines the appropriate medical decision

GENERAL

How much does a consultation cost?

Each consultation costs $49.99 with no hidden fees.

GENERAL

When do I pay?​

Payment is completed after your consultation and before any prescription or medical documents are sent.

GENERAL

Who reviews my case?

All cases are reviewed by licensed physicians with decades of medical experience who have treated thousands of patients.

GENERAL

Can I start the consultation without giving my name?

Yes. You can begin by describing your symptoms without sharing your name or ID

GENERAL

Will I need to provide identification?

In some cases, identification may be required before prescriptions or medical documents are issued.

GENERAL

Will the support team see my ID, photos, or videos?

No. Images, videos, or identification you send are only reviewed by the licensed physician. The support team cannot view these files unless you choose to allow them to access them to help continue your consultation.

GENERAL

Are my photos or medical information secure?

Yes. Any images, videos, or identification you send are securely reviewed by the licensed medical professional handling your case.

GENERAL

How long does it take to receive my prescription or documents?

Prescriptions, lab requisitions, or imaging requests are usually sent within a few hours after the consultation.

GENERAL

What services can Treedi provide?​

If medically appropriate, physicians can provide prescriptions, medication renewals, laboratory requisitions, imaging requests, and general medical guidance

GENERAL

Can I get a prescription online?​

Yes, if medically appropriate after review by a licensed physician.

GENERAL

Can I request lab tests?​

Yes. Physicians can issue requisitions for blood tests and other laboratory investigations when medically appropriate.

GENERAL

Can I request imaging tests?​​

Yes. Imaging requests such as X-rays, ultrasounds, CT scans, or MRIs may be issued if medically necessary.

GENERAL

Can Treedi treat infections?​​

Yes. Many common non-urgent conditions such as infections, respiratory issues, skin conditions, or minor health concerns can be assessed.

GENERAL

Is Treedi a hospital?​​​

No. Treedi is a private medical clinic for non-urgent care.

GENERAL

Is Treedi covered by OHIP or RAMQ?​

No. Treedi is a private clinic, and consultations cost $49.99.

GENERAL

Who can use Treedi?​​​

Treedi is available for adults 18+ located in Ontario or Quebec

GENERAL

Can I use Treedi if I am traveling?​​​​

Yes, as long as you are currently located in Ontario or Quebec during the consultation.

GENERAL

What if I have a medical emergency?

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department immediately.

GENERAL

Where is your clinic located?​

Our clinic is located at 343 Preston St, Ottawa, ON K1S 1N4, Canada. Online consultations are also available.
